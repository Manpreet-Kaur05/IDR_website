01 / About IDR

Built for the
Realities
of Risk.

Digital Risk Cyber Risk AI Risk Technology Risk Governance

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments. We exist because the gap between academic understanding and operational readiness has never been wider — and the stakes have never been higher.

Our programmes are designed with practitioners, for practitioners. Through a formal partnership with a leading UK university, IDR bridges rigorous academic insight with applied, real-world projects — equipping every graduate to contribute from day one in financial services, critical infrastructure and beyond.

"IDR doesn't just train talent — we shape risk professionals capable of leading in an era defined by AI, interconnected systems and evolving regulatory demands."

02 / Our Model

Three
Core
Pillars.

IDR operates across training, innovation and advisory — an integrated model designed to serve individuals and organisations at every stage of the risk journey.

Pillar 01

Academy

Intensive bootcamps, structured pathways and continuing professional development for students entering the field and practitioners seeking to upskill. Delivered in partnership with UK academia and shaped by industry need.

Bootcamps CPD Apprenticeship Certification
Pillar 02

Innovation & Incubation

Developing proprietary intellectual property, next-generation risk models and AI governance frameworks. IDR's incubation arm supports emerging risk methodologies and equips organisations to navigate the AI-regulatory frontier.

AI Governance Risk IP R&D Frameworks
Pillar 03

Advisory Services

Helping regulated organisations implement leading risk and security frameworks — including NIST CSF, ISO 27001 and NIS2 — through expert consultation, gap analysis and pragmatic implementation support.

NIST CSF ISO 27001 NIS2 Compliance
